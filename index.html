Hotkey tips
  • Must include at least one modifier (⌘ / ⌃ / ⌥ / ⇧) plus another key.
  • Avoid common app shortcuts like ⌘S / ⌘C / ⌘V — they'd be hijacked everywhere.
  • If a combo is already used by another app or macOS, Restash will refuse it.
