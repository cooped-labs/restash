Restash — Stash cycler

Tap V or click Next · auto-locks after 600ms

Cursor-summoned numpad with paged stashes. Hold ⌘⇧ and tap V repeatedly to cycle. Release the modifiers to lock the visible stash and use number keys 1–9 to paste.

How it works

  1. Press ⌘⇧V anywhere — numpad opens at your cursor on your default stash.
  2. While still holding ⌘⇧, tap V again to cycle to the next stash. The grid slides; the name + dots update.
  3. Release ⌘⇧ to lock the visible stash.
  4. Press 1–9 (or click a tile) to paste.
Auto-lock fallback: if no V press for 600 ms, we treat the modifiers as released and lock the current stash. Avoids needing a low-level modifier listener.
Items can live in multiple stashes — your ETH wallet might appear in both Personal and Crypto. A "Default" stash auto-contains everything so power users never lose anything.
