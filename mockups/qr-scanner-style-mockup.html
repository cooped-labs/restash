My pick

Style B — Square Viewfinder. The QR shape and the lens shape match, the corner-bracket pattern is universally readable as "scanner," and dropping the sweep line keeps it clean. Style C is the close runner-up if you want maximum refinement; Style A is fine but reads slightly clinical; Style D is striking but probably too loud for the rest of the app's tone.
