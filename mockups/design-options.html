Restash — Quick-pick modal designs

Same data, same hotkey, four different feels. Pick one (or mix elements).
Option A
Pill Cloud
Free-flowing chips, label-only. Click anywhere on a pill to copy. Feels like a tag cloud — informal, scannable at a glance.
+Highest density — 8+ items in tiny space.
+Each item self-sized; long labels still readable.
−No live search; no keyboard nav. Pure mouse.
−Layout shifts as items are added/removed.
Option B
Numpad 3×3
Top 9 most-used items as a phone-keypad grid. 1–9 on your keyboard to copy without moving the mouse. Best for a curated set.

+Fixed footprint — never grows.

−Labels truncate hard at this size.

Option D
Dock Strip
Horizontal row of icon-only tiles, like a tiny floating Dock. Labels reveal on hover. The most compact option — designed to live one cursor-flick from anywhere.
+Tiniest footprint — single 60px-tall strip.
+Familiar Dock metaphor; macOS-native feel.
−Icon-only = must memorize what each slot holds.
−Lots of items → horizontal scroll, awkward at cursor.
