My recommendation: Option 1 (Two-line). The Restash use case is "I have an exact thing in mind and need to find it" — the bold label + monospaced value preview is the right shape for that. Density (Option 2) matters less than read-confidence, and the metadata in Option 3 starts to clutter what should be a quick lookup. Option 1's ~40px row + 320px window still gives you ~6 visible without scrolling, which covers most use cases.
